Welcome to Better Bike ABQ

The question seemed simple, where are the safest roads in Albuquerque to ride a
bike? As a bicycle enthusiast it was my personal interests that gave the spark for this
project. It started as a class project and grew into this website. With Much help from
Jennifer Buntz of the Duke City Wheelmen I was able to build several maps displaying
bicycle related accidents in the ABQ metro area. What I discovered was there are many
areas of town that boost a high incident of accidents. The factors for these accidents
are many, as it is difficult to surmise only one definitive contributing factor. This
site hopes to highlight Danger Zones in the ABQ metro area as well as provide some sage
advice for best riding practices in the city. It is my hope to improve ridership as a
whole and to keep both riders and drivers safe. Enjoy!
